The Best Films I Remember

The 3 films I have seen recently which stayed with me

Suzhou River

Guerilla style idiosyncratic film-making, engaging, and heart-breaking, love story, yet never lets the viewer settle.

Drive My Car

Beautiful, understated, story about grief, guilt, and companionship. Says a lot with just enough words and no more.

Lady MacBeth

Sublime performance from Florence Pugh in her "breakout" role. Claustrophobic, uncomfortable, unnerving, without ever having to utilise too much viscera.
